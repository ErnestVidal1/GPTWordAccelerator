Initial Setup & Philosophy

Define Needs & Search

Clearly define your document layout automation needs and search your XML macro repository. Use ChatGPT with our filters to find base macros from successful past projects that might serve as a template.

Communicate & Develop

Use our structured prompts to communicate your needs and any existing macro code similar to the current case to ChatGPT. Plan the development, act as a tester, provide feedback on adjustments and errors, and execute the macro in the document when ready.

Finalize & Archive

With ChatGPT's help, generate an XML structure with metadata, descriptions, tags, and categories for easy future retrieval and archive it efficiently.
